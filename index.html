Why waits happen
The waiting room isn't a line. Here's how it really works.
1

Based on need, not arrival time

Patients are seen by how urgent their condition is. Someone who arrives after you may be seen first if they're more critical, and the same protects you.

2

Your place can change

If your priority is re-assessed, or a space opens, your status here updates automatically.

3

The team is working even when it's quiet

Behind the doors, clinicians are treating patients, waiting on tests, and freeing up rooms.

Our aim: make the system readable to you, not to overwhelm you with everything at once.
